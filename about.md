# What is Pipeline Engineer?

Pipeline Engineer is a [QGIS](https://qgis.org/) plugin that streamlines the design of pipe systems.

A pipe network can be modelled, tested and eventually broken down fitting by fitting from points and lines on a map.

Case studies and applications can be found on the Pipeline Engineer [blog](/blog/).

## Mission

To deliver pipeline projects in a safe and efficent manner.

With so many moving parts, changing inputs and outputs, it can get very easy to lose track of where things are at.

The goal of this plugin is to systemise the design process, freeing up room for teams to focus more on the important decisions.
